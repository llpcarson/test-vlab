General Algorithm

  1. Compute wet bulb temperature
  2. call calwxt() to compute instantaneous precipitation type.
  3. For Ramer algorithm, call calwxt_ramer()
  4. For Bourgouin algorithm, call calwxt_bourg()
  5. For revised NCEP algorithm, call calwxt_revised()
  6. For explicit algorithm(n3dfercld=3,ferrier's scheme), call calwxt_explicit()
  7. call calwxt_dominant() to compute dominant precip type
