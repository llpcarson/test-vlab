10 !!!!! sw-rrtm3 radiation package description !!!!!
11 !!!!! ============================================================== !!!!!
12 ! !
13 ! this package includes ncep's modifications of the rrtm-sw radiation !
14 ! code from aer inc. !
15 ! !
16 ! the sw-rrtm3 package includes these parts: !
17 ! !
18 ! 'radsw_rrtm3_param.f' !
19 ! 'radsw_rrtm3_datatb.f' !
20 ! 'radsw_rrtm3_main.f' !
21 ! !
22 ! the 'radsw_rrtm3_param.f' contains: !
23 ! !
24 ! 'module_radsw_parameters' -- band parameters set up !
25 ! !
26 ! the 'radsw_rrtm3_datatb.f' contains: !
27 ! !
28 ! 'module_radsw_ref' -- reference temperature and pressure !
29 ! 'module_radsw_cldprtb' -- cloud property coefficients table !
30 ! 'module_radsw_sflux' -- spectral distribution of solar flux !
31 ! 'module_radsw_kgbnn' -- absorption coeffients for 14 !
32 ! bands, where nn = 16-29 !
33 ! !
34 ! the 'radsw_rrtm3_main.f' contains: !
35 ! !
36 ! 'module_radsw_main' -- main sw radiation transfer !
37 ! !
38 ! in the main module 'module_radsw_main' there are only two !
39 ! externally callable subroutines: !
40 ! !
41 ! 'swrad' -- main rrtm3 sw radiation routine !
42 ! 'rswinit' -- initialization routine !
43 ! !
44 ! all the sw radiation subprograms become contained subprograms !
45 ! in module 'module_radsw_main' and many of them are not directly !
46 ! accessable from places outside the module. !
47 ! !
48 ! compilation sequence is: !
49 ! !
50 ! 'radsw_rrtm3_param.f' !
51 ! 'radsw_rrtm3_datatb.f' !
52 ! 'radsw_rrtm3_main.f' !
53 ! !
54 ! and all should be put in front of routines that use sw modules !
55 ! !
56 ! ncep modifications history log: !
57 ! !
58 ! see list in program "radsw_rrtm3_main.f" !
59 ! !
